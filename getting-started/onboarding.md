# Onboarding Guide

This guide outlines the steps required to gain access to the Canton-based Tokenization Platform and begin issuing and managing tokenized assets.

---

## Overview

Access to the platform is provisioned by the platform operator.  
Each user account is securely created and linked to one or more Canton `PartyId`(s).

---

## Step 1: Request Account Creation

To begin onboarding, please contact the team:

- 📧 Email: [your-email@example.com]  
- ✈️ Telegram: [telegram]

Provide the following information:

- Organization name
- PartyId(s) for token issuance control

---

## Step 2: Receive Setup Link

Once your request is approved:

- A user account will be created with a temporary default credential.
- A secure setup link will be sent to your registered email address.
- You will be required to set a new password before first login.

For security reasons, the setup link may expire after a limited time.

---

## Step 3: Link PartyId(s)

After logging in:

- You may link one or multiple Canton `PartyId`s to your account.
- A single account can manage multiple PartyIds depending on your operational requirements.
- Each PartyId represents a ledger identity used for token issuance and transaction execution.

If you require assistance creating or registering new PartyIds, contact the team.

---

## Step 4: Password Reset

If you forget your password:

1. Contact the team via email or phone.
2. A new secure setup link will be sent to your registered email.
3. Use the link to reset your password.

---

## Step 5: Platform Access Ready

Once your account is created and PartyId(s) are linked:

You are ready to use the Tokenization Platform.

You may proceed to:

- Create and register tokenized instruments
- Configure token metadata and supply parameters
- Execute minting and transfer workflows
- Manage lifecycle operations
